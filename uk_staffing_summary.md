## 🇬🇧 UK/IE GSO Staffing — Adding 18 Sales Reps

**Recommendation: 15 GSO reps total (14 current + 1 additional)**

---

### Current State
- **162 DSRs/month** across 87 active UK AEs (~1.9 DSRs per AE/month)
- **14 GSO reps** currently supporting UK/IE
- **22-day median** go-live time | 39% completing in ≤14 days
- Sufficient HID coverage in UK/IE

### Impact of +18 AEs
- Projected volume: **~189 DSRs/month** (+27/mo at 1.5 DSRs per new AE)
- Required throughput at 15 reps: **12.6 completions/rep/month**
- This is achievable — our top performers already hit 13–19/month

### Why 15 (not 14, not 16)
- **14 works on paper** (11.8/rep/mo needed) but leaves zero buffer for PTO, ramp, or uneven distribution
- **15 gives us a comfortable margin** at 12.6/rep/mo — well within reach as we push productivity improvements and move toward the 14-day go-live model
- **16 is not needed** given current AE submission rates and the performance conversations already underway
- The additional rep also provides coverage as we hold reps accountable to higher throughput standards

### Key Levers to Hit Target
1. Same-day outreach on new DSRs
2. Book all appointments during kickoff call
3. Eliminate "Missing Info" blockers with complete DSR submissions from AEs
4. Target: 22d median → 16d median (28% improvement)

|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **Current (no change)** | 162 | 14 | 11.6 | Tight |
| **With +18 AEs** | 189 | 15 | 12.6 | ✅ Manageable |

**Bottom line:** 15 reps handles the growth with room to absorb productivity improvements. The 14-day go-live playbook is the force multiplier — it turns a tight staffing model into one with headroom.
