1
Pre-Sales: Site Readiness
AE owns — happens during the sales cycle, before DSR creation
Before Day 0
AE
Account Executive
Trigger Site Survey During Proposal Stage
Once deal confidence is >70%, dispatch a site survey through the partner network. This runs in parallel with contract negotiation — no time added to the sales cycle.
  • Request site survey via partner dispatch (Sora Partners, Absolute POS, Tech Pantry, Solio Marlow)
  • Share seller contact info + business address with partner
  • Target: survey completed 1-2 weeks before expected close
⏱ Survey must be complete before DSR creation
AE
Account Executive
Capture Site Readiness Checklist
During your on-site visit or seller conversations, collect the information GSO needs to plan the full onboarding. This takes 5 minutes and saves 10+ days.
  • Number of POS stations and locations for each
  • Ethernet available at each station? Or WiFi only?
  • Current POS system (for data migration planning)
  • Preferred install date and time window
  • Any construction, renovation, or opening date constraints
  • Key contact name + phone for scheduling
P
Implementation Partner
Complete Site Survey + Flag Cabling Needs
Partner conducts the physical site assessment. If cabling is needed (5% of sites), schedule it immediately — don't wait for post-close.
  • Assess network infrastructure (drops, WiFi, router capacity)
  • Map terminal placement and cable run lengths
  • Confirm power outlet availability
  • If cabling needed → schedule pre-close (saves 8 additional days)
  • Upload survey results to DSR/deal record
2
Day 0–1: Kickoff & Schedule Everything
GSO Rep owns — single call that sets the entire timeline
Day 0–1
G
GSO Rep
Same-Day Seller Outreach
Contact the seller within hours of DSR assignment — not days. Use the contact info the AE captured. Goal: schedule the kickoff call for today or tomorrow.
  • Call or text seller within 4 hours of assignment
  • Introduce yourself and confirm you have their site survey results
  • Book kickoff call for same day or next business day
⏱ Outreach within 4 hours · Kickoff booked within 24 hours
G
GSO Rep
Kickoff Call — Book ALL Appointments (30 min)
This is the most important call in the process. In 30 minutes, you set the entire onboarding timeline. The seller leaves this call knowing exactly what happens and when.
  • Review site survey results with seller — confirm layout and hardware plan
  • Walk through the onboarding timeline: "Here's what the next 10 days look like"
  • Book Menu Review Call (Day 2-3)
  • Book Dashboard Training (Day 3-4)
  • Book Installation date (Day 5-7) — dispatch partner DSA immediately
  • Book POS Training (Day 7-8, in-person for Onsite, remote for Premium)
  • Book SO Training Call (Day 8-9)
  • Book Go-Live Support if applicable (Day 9-10)
  • Confirm hardware shipping address and expected delivery
  • Send seller a calendar summary of all booked sessions
⏱ All appointments booked before hanging up · Partner DSA dispatched same day
G
GSO Rep
Dispatch All Partner Activities Immediately
Don't wait for training stages to complete before dispatching partner work. Send all DSAs now so partners can schedule around the seller's confirmed availability.
  • Dispatch Installation DSA with confirmed date from kickoff call
  • Dispatch Go-Live Support DSA if applicable
  • Dispatch any remaining field service DSAs
  • Include site survey results in dispatch notes
3
Days 2–10: Parallel Execution
GSO Rep + Partners — remote training and field work run simultaneously
Day 2–10
G
GSO Rep
Day 2–3: Menu Review Call
Review and finalize the seller's menu configuration in Square. This is remote and can happen while the partner is preparing for installation.
  • Review menu items, categories, modifiers
  • Set up pricing and tax configuration
  • Configure discounts and service charges if needed
G
GSO Rep
Day 3–4: Dashboard Training
Remote session walking the seller through the Square Dashboard — reports, team management, settings.
  • Dashboard overview: sales, reports, team management
  • Device management and settings
  • Online ordering setup if applicable
P
Implementation Partner
Day 5–7: Hardware Installation
Partner arrives on-site for hardware installation. Site survey is already done, cabling is already complete, hardware has been shipped — this is pure execution.
  • Install all POS terminals at surveyed locations
  • Connect to network (ethernet or WiFi per survey)
  • Configure kitchen printers and receipt printers
  • Test all hardware connections
  • Brief walkthrough with seller on hardware basics
G
GSO Rep
Day 7–8: POS Training
Hands-on POS training — in-person for Onsite (day after install), remote for Premium/Plus. Seller and staff learn to use the system with their actual menu and hardware.
  • Order entry, payment processing, refunds
  • Table management (if restaurant)
  • End-of-day procedures
  • Staff training on daily operations
G
GSO Rep
Day 8–9: SO Training Call
Square Online training — website setup, online ordering configuration, marketing tools.
  • Square Online store setup
  • Online ordering configuration
  • Marketing and loyalty tools overview
P
Implementation Partner
Day 9–10: Go-Live Support (if applicable)
Partner on-site for the seller's first live service day. Troubleshoot any issues in real-time, ensure smooth transition from old POS.
  • On-site support during first live service
  • Real-time troubleshooting
  • Final hardware adjustments
  • Confirm seller confidence before departing
4
Day 10–14: Go-Live & Close Out
GSO Rep owns — confirm success and mark complete
Day 10–14
G
GSO Rep
Post-Go-Live Check-In
Quick call or text 1-2 days after go-live. Confirm the seller is processing transactions successfully and address any remaining questions.
  • Verify transactions are processing
  • Check for any hardware or connectivity issues
  • Answer remaining questions
  • Confirm seller is confident operating independently
G
GSO Rep
Mark Implementation Complete
Update the DSR status to Implementation Complete. Ensure all DSA activities are marked as completed. Trigger CSAT survey.
  • Update DSR status → Implementation Complete
  • Verify all partner DSAs are closed
  • CSAT survey triggered automatically
  • Handoff to post-implementation support if needed
⏱ DSR closed within 14 days of creation
